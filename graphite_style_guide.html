01 — Foundation
Color
Eight core palette colors and a neutral ramp. Use terracotta (#D76B5B) for primary CTAs and interactions. Ice (#ECF2F9) for panels and info surfaces. Red (#E21837) strictly for errors and destructive actions. Two secondary accents supplement the palette: Blue Accent (#1565c0) for checkbox fills and focus rings; Warning Amber (#f9a825) for warning icons on trip rows.

✓ Do
  • Use grey logo on white, smoke (#F2F2F2), and ice (#ECF2F9) backgrounds
  • Use white logo on black, charcoal, terracotta, and graphite backgrounds
  • Maintain minimum logo height of 36px (md)
  • Keep clear space equal to the height of the "f" mark on all sides
✕ Don't
  • Don't place the grey logo on dark or colored backgrounds
  • Don't place the white logo on terracotta — the orange "f" mark lacks sufficient contrast
  • Don't stretch, rotate, or recolor the wordmark
  • Don't use the logo below 36px height
  • Don't place on low-contrast backgrounds that reduce legibility

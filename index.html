01Operating PrinciplesDOC / PHILOSOPHY

Technology only matters when it solves a real problem for a real person. The rest of my job is making that obvious.

P-01

Earn the conversation

Getting the meeting isn't the same as being trusted. I do the homework, learn their world fast, and say so when something won't work.

P-02

Prove it

Feature tours don't close deals. A tight proof of concept on the customer's own data does.

P-03

Show, don't tell

People can't approve what they can't picture. I draw the system — diagrams, demos, working tools — so everyone in the room sees the same thing.
